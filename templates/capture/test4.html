Regulations when taking photos for facial recognition

To ensure the facial recognition system works effectively, the following regulations must be followed:

  1. Image quality: Photos must be clear, not blurred or bright.
  2. Lighting: Even light source, avoid shadows and do not shoot against the light.
  3. Face position: Face in the middle of the photo, not obscured.
  4. Expression: Natural, eyes open, mouth closed.
  5. Shooting angle: Shoot straight, do not tilt the head.
  6. Clothes: Simple, avoid too many accessories.
  7. Background: Should be simple, avoid too many distracting details.

Complying with these regulations helps improve the accuracy of facial recognition systems.
