Regulations when taking photos for facial recognition

To ensure the facial recognition system works effectively, follow these rules:

  1. Image quality: Clear photos, not blurry.
  2. Lighting: Even light, no shadows, and avoid shooting against the light.
  3. Face position: Center the face, unobstructed.
  4. Expression: Neutral, eyes open, mouth closed.
  5. Shooting angle: Shoot straight, no tilting.
  6. Clothes: Simple, no excessive accessories.
  7. Background: Simple, without too many distracting details.

Following these rules improves the system's accuracy.
